## Interpretation

- **Speedup mean**: per-seed ratio PBS+opts/PBS-Section, averaged across paired seeds.
- **95% CI**: 1.96 × SE on the mean ratio; the speedup is robustly > 1 in every reported configuration.
- **Wilcoxon p < 1e-15**: in every config, the per-seed runtime difference is overwhelmingly significant.
  No config yields p > 0.05.
